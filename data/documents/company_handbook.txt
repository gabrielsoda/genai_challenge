ACME Corporation Employee Handbook

Welcome to ACME Corporation!

Chapter 1: Working Hours

Our standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees are entitled to a one-hour lunch break. Flexible working arrangements may be available upon manager approval.

Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week after completing the 90-day probationary period. All remote work arrangements must be documented and approved by your direct supervisor.

Chapter 2: Leave Policy

Annual Leave:
- All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days

Sick Leave:
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year

Parental Leave:
- Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave
- Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid parental leave
- Parental leave must be taken within the first 12 months of the child's birth or adoption

Chapter 3: Compensation

Salaries are paid on the 15th and last day of each month via direct deposit. Annual salary reviews occur in January, with any adjustments effective February 1st.

Performance bonuses are discretionary and based on individual and company performance. Bonus decisions are typically communicated in March.
