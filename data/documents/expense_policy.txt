ACME Expense Reimbursement Policy

General Guidelines

All business expenses must be:
- Reasonable and necessary for business purposes
- Pre-approved for amounts over $500
- Submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense
- Accompanied by itemized receipts

Travel Expenses

Airfare:
- Book economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use the company's preferred travel agency

Hotels:
- Maximum rate: $200/night (domestic), $300/night (international)
- Choose hotels with corporate rates when available
- Incidentals are the employee's responsibility

Ground Transportation:
- Use ride-sharing services or public transit when practical
- Rental cars require manager approval
- Economy or compact cars only

Meals and Entertainment

Per Diem Rates (no receipts required up to these amounts):
- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $50

Client Entertainment:
- Pre-approval required for expenses over $200
- Must document business purpose and attendees
- Alcohol limited to reasonable amounts

Home Office Expenses

Remote employees may expense:
- Internet service (up to $75/month)
- Office supplies (up to $200/year)
- Ergonomic equipment (one-time $500 allowance)

How to Submit Expenses

1. Log into Expensify (company account)
2. Create a new expense report
3. Upload receipts via mobile app or email
4. Categorize each expense appropriately
5. Add business justification
6. Submit for manager approval

Reimbursement is processed within 5 business days of approval.
