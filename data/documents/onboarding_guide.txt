ACME New Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Team!

Your First Day

1. Arrive at 9:00 AM at the main reception
2. HR will provide your employee badge and welcome kit
3. IT will set up your laptop and accounts
4. Meet your manager and team members
5. Complete required compliance training

First Week Checklist

□ Complete all HR paperwork
□ Set up email and communication tools
□ Review the Employee Handbook
□ Complete security awareness training
□ Schedule 1:1 meetings with team members
□ Set up direct deposit for payroll
□ Choose your benefits (within 30 days)

Benefits Enrollment

You must enroll in benefits within 30 days of your start date. Options include:
- Health insurance (company pays 80% of premiums)
- Dental and vision insurance
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Life insurance (1x annual salary included)
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Training Resources

All employees have access to:
- ACME Learning Portal (online courses)
- LinkedIn Learning subscription
- $1,500 annual professional development budget
- Internal mentorship program
