ACME Security and Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Data Collection

ACME collects the following types of data:
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage data (features used, session duration)
- Technical data (IP address, device type, browser)
- Payment information (processed securely by our payment partners)

2. Data Protection

All data is encrypted:
- In transit: TLS 1.3 encryption
- At rest: AES-256 encryption

Our data centers are SOC 2 Type II certified and located in:
- United States (primary)
- European Union (for EU customers)
- Australia (for APAC customers)

3. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction
- Request data deletion
- Export your data in portable format
- Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@acme.com.

4. Password Requirements

Passwords must:
- Be at least 12 characters long
- Contain uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character
- Not be a previously used password

5. Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is:
- Optional for personal accounts
- Required for Business and Enterprise accounts
- Supported via SMS, authenticator apps, and hardware keys

6. Compliance

ACME is compliant with:
- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California)
- SOC 2 Type II
- ISO 27001
- HIPAA (for Healthcare customers with BAA)
