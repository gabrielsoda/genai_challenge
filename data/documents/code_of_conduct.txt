ACME Code of Conduct

Our Values

At ACME, we are committed to:
- Integrity in all our dealings
- Respect for all individuals
- Excellence in our work
- Innovation and continuous improvement
- Social responsibility

Workplace Behavior

We expect all employees to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain a harassment-free workplace
- Communicate professionally at all times
- Support diversity and inclusion
- Report concerns without fear of retaliation

Conflicts of Interest

Employees must:
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest
- Not engage in business with competitors without approval
- Not accept gifts valued over $100 from vendors
- Avoid nepotism in hiring and promotion decisions

Confidentiality

Employees must protect confidential information including:
- Trade secrets and proprietary technology
- Customer data and personal information
- Financial information and projections
- Strategic plans and business decisions

Confidentiality obligations continue after employment ends.

Reporting Violations

Report violations through:
- Your direct manager
- Human Resources
- Ethics hotline: 1-800-ACME-ETH (anonymous)
- ethics@acme.com

ACME prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports concerns in good faith.

Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Performance improvement plan
- Suspension
- Termination
- Legal action where appropriate
