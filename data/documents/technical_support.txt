Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Application won't start
Solution:
1. Restart your computer
2. Ensure your system meets minimum requirements
3. Check for pending updates
4. Reinstall the application if issues persist

Issue: Login problems
Solution:
1. Verify your username and password
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try the "Forgot Password" option
4. Contact support if account is locked

Issue: Sync not working
Solution:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify you're logged into the same account on all devices
3. Check available cloud storage space
4. Force sync from Settings > Sync > Force Sync
