ACME Corporation Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. General Refund Terms

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason. After 30 days, refunds are provided at our discretion and may be subject to a 15% restocking fee.

Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded or activated unless they are defective.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:
1. Log into your account at acme.com/myaccount
2. Navigate to Order History
3. Select the order and click "Request Refund"
4. Provide a reason for the refund request

Alternatively, contact our support team at support@acme.com or call 1-800-ACME-HELP.

3. Processing Time

- Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
- PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
- Bank transfer refunds: 7-10 business days

4. Exceptions

The following items are not eligible for refunds:
- Customized or personalized products
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Gift cards
- Subscription services after the first billing cycle

5. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a full refund or replacement at no additional cost. Please include photos of the damage when contacting support.
